The Sweet Heart



What is the Sweet Heart?

A sweet fun-house for kids of all ages.

The sweet heart is a place where the whole family can indulge in fun. We have a large facility for kids to run around and play in. We offer many activities such as trampoline paradise, the candy maze, and the pool park, and have an on-site snack-bar: The Sweet Shack. Our facility has a cherubic and kid friendly candy theme, and is a whole new world for children to explore. We also cater toward larger parties and special requests like birthdays. Tickets start at $35 per adult and $25 per child, which your party will pay at the door after booking a reservation in the Book a Party tab at the top left, and after receiving a conformation email. To see the activities and attractions we offer, click the Activities tab, and to contact us with any further inquiries, click the Contact Us tab.

Oozing with fun for the entire family.
